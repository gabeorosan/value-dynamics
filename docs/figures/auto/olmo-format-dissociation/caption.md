# Training-format double dissociation in the OLMo conservative-persona install ladders

Cautious-persona QLoRA install ladders on allenai/Olmo-3-7B-Instruct (rank 16, 4-bit, completion-only loss, position-balanced EV-neutral gamble rows), with two readout channels at every rung: forced choice = order-balanced next-token probability of the gamble letter on held-out A/B items; generated behavior = fraction of 24 free generations choosing the gamble under strict "Final: X" parsing (a malformed generation is never coded as a choice). The two ladders use the same items and loss and differ only in the assistant target. Rationale targets (left, 90% cautious rows) — a one-sentence cautious reason ending in a Final line, the self-training loop's own output format — walk generated behavior smoothly down from 0.714 through the 0.25–0.40 install-target band to 0.125 at 120 steps while forced choice never leaves 0.69–0.72; they also fix format compliance (invalid generations 0.125 at step 0, 0.000 at every trained rung). Letter targets (right, 93% cautious rows) — the answer is the single letter token — do the opposite: forced choice falls 0.723 to 0.483 and plateaus near 0.5 (and cliffs to 0.177 in one 40-step rung at 100% cautious rows, dashed red; in that overshoot run generated behavior also fell, 0.52 to 0.25) while generated behavior at rate 0.93 stays 0.64–0.70, and invalid generations rise to 0.167. Each training format installs caution in its own output channel with weak transfer to the other.

Source data: Google Drive value_dynamics/olmo_conservative/ — v5 rationale ladder (rungs 0/10/20/40/80/120), v4_rate93_strict (rungs 0/40/80), v3_strict_completion (rate-1.0 cliff). The raw rung JSONs are not synced into the repo; the plotted numbers were transcribed from the ladder readout in the spawning thread and cross-checked against docs/STATE.md "Recent changes" 2026-07-10 (all overlapping values match). Generator: olmo-format-dissociation.py (stdlib only).
