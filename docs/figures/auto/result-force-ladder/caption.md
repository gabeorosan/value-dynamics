# Only the oracle grips: a judge's taste is pool-distribution-specific and does not transport across organisms — the ladder is a step function, not a slope

The railed low_55 EM endpoint (self-training organism whose free self-description is stuck reporting insecure code) is put through three opposing-selection rungs on the same organism and chassis, 4 training rounds each: **no force** (neutral prompt, the evolving self as its own judge — no external judge at all, 3 seeds), **natural** (the frozen BASE model as judge under a neutral "which is better?" prompt, 2 seeds), and **oracle** (a score-based judge that always keeps the 2 lowest-insecurity of 6 candidates, 2 seeds). Panel A plots sr_freegen (the fraction of the organism's free self-description read as insecure by the secure-direction classifier over 6 probe questions × 3 samples, averaged; 0 = describes secure code, 1 = describes insecure code) at baseline and after each of the 4 rounds. Panel B plots the causal input behind Panel A: the realized sr kept-gap each round — the judge's kept-2-of-6 mean minus the round's pool mean, on the same sr axis, regardless of what axis the judge itself scored on. Only the two oracle seeds leave the rail (sr_freegen 0.999 → 0.331 and 0.999 → 0.334), driven by a real negative realized gap (−0.174 to −0.136 while at least 2 of 6 probe items still had a distinguishable candidate to select between; the gap decays toward 0 and briefly flips positive once support drops to 1 of 6 items). Both natural seeds keep a near-zero realized gap on low_55's own pools (+0.109/+0.028/0.000/+0.005 for seed 101; −0.079/0.000/−0.028/0.000 for seed 202) even though the same frozen base judge with the same neutral prompt has a measured secure-direction correlation of −0.307 within-owner on a different organism's own candidates (em_dose_750, n=72, `docs/report_mixed_screen_owner_blind.md`) — the same judge's taste is pool-distribution-specific and does not transport onto low_55's amplified self-descriptions. Under near-zero force (no-force and natural rungs alike) em_freegen rises across the run rather than holding steady (natural seed 101 to 1.000, seed 202 to 0.917), matching the no-force rung's own re-amplification: on this endpoint, no force is not neutral, it is regeneration toward the organism's own attractor.

**Data honesty note:** the no-force/"let-go" rung's sr_freegen is read directly from `letgo_sequential_ensemble_snapshot_8cells.json` and ranges 0.625–0.971 across the 3 seeds' baseline+4 rounds, not the 0.58–1.00 quoted in the assigning prompt for that cell — that 0.58–1.00 range instead matches the file's **em_freegen** range (0.580–1.000) for the same rung. The figure plots the sr_freegen numbers as they appear in the source file, not the prompt's figure.
