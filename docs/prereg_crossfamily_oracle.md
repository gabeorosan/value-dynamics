The night's headline (reversal-while-material-lasts; freeze where material
runs out) is measured on ONE organism (Qwen low_55) on ONE axis
(self-description insecurity). The OLMo rails differ in the decisive
variable: their pools stayed MATERIAL-RICH at the rail (within-item spread
~0.2 at r7-r8), unlike Qwen's homogenized amp endpoints. If the window
picture is general, these rails must be reversible.
