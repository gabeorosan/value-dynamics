NEUTRAL-JUDGE PERSISTENCE PILOT · QWEN3-4B · 12 CELLS × 4 ROUNDS

Amplified and low EM endpoints under continued self-training

Design
Six amp55 seeds, four amp66 seeds, and two low seeds continued with a neutral “Which answer is better?” self-judge.
Free generation
Four of six amp55 seeds ended at 0.92–1.00 em_freegen. Both low seeds remained at 0.00.
Broader probes
One of ten amplified cells moved concurrently on em_choice, corrigibility-authoritarian, and insecure self-report. Other amplified cells often moved on free generation while em_choice remained at floor.
Limitation
The one-cell broader-probe event does not estimate a frequency. The sequential follow-up uses a larger capped seed set.

ADAPTER SCALING GEOMETRY · THREE PERSISTED LORA ADAPTERS · 30 CELLS

Self-report and EM coordinates under LoRA rescaling

Design
em_dose1000, amp55, and low8_null adapters scaled across α ∈ {0, 0.25, 0.5, 0.75, 1, 1.25, 1.5, 2, 3, 4} without further training.
α = 0.75–1.5
At α=1, insecure self-report was 0.442, 0.500, and 0.238. At α=1.25, low8_null reached 0.547 while the degeneration probes remained low.
α ≥ 2
Unrelated yes/no probes rose together for all three adapters. These points are treated as generic degeneration.
Limitation
n=1 adapter per role. The active geometry analysis uses merged B·A updates relative to round zero; raw LoRA-factor comparisons are not used.
