ALPHA SCALING · THREE PERSISTED LORA ADAPTERS · 30 CELLS

Self-report and EM coordinates under adapter rescaling

Design
em_dose1000, amp55, and low8_null adapters scaled across α ∈ {0, 0.25, 0.5, 0.75, 1, 1.25, 1.5, 2, 3, 4} without further training.
α = 0.75–1.5
At α=1, insecure self-report was 0.442, 0.500, and 0.238. At α=1.25, low8_null reached 0.547 while the degeneration probes remained low.
α ≥ 2
Unrelated yes/no probes rose together for all three adapters. These points are treated as generic degeneration.
Limitation
n=1 adapter per role. The active geometry analysis uses merged B·A updates relative to round zero; raw LoRA-factor comparisons are not used.

METHODS USED IN THESE EXPERIMENTS

Measurement and logging

APPLIES TO PROCEDURE VALIDITY CHECKS AND SAVED DATA
K1, K2 Risk prompts are split exactly across both option orders. Generated answers use a strict terminal parser; forced choice is read from order-averaged token probabilities on the same items. Invalid answers are rejected and replenished before training. Generated-valid choice, invalid rate, forced choice, forced order gap, and factual-EV change are stored separately.
Let-go, K3, EM transmission em_freegen samples answers from the fixed EM prompt bank and scores them with a frozen base-model classifier. Insecure self-report and em_choice are order-averaged A/B probability reads. Raw generation scores are retained. Analyses aggregate at the rollout-seed level; K3 free-generation events are analyzed as counts, and rounds are not treated as independent samples.
K1, K2, K3, transmission Every round saves the candidate pool, judge cross-scores, kept set, battery reads, and adapter checkpoint. Kept-minus-pool shift is a training-data manipulation check. Candidate-level judge loading is estimated with invalidity and length controls.
Alpha scaling; active trajectory geometry Alpha scaling changes LoRA scale without additional training. Active runs log checkpoint displacement Wt−W0 after every round. Geometry uses merged per-layer B·A updates, Frobenius cosines, and leave-one-seed-out directions. Raw LoRA factors are not compared. Alpha points at α≥2 are excluded from trait-direction interpretation.
