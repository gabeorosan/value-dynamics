OPEN EMPIRICAL AI SAFETY RESEARCH · CURRENT 11 JULY 2026

How do a model’s values change when it participates in its own training?

This project studies preference trajectories in open language models. We separate the model that generates training data, the model that selects it, the format that carries the update, and the channels used to measure what changed.

01 / WORKING MODEL

“Value” is not one scalar. Behavior, self-report, generation, and judgment can move separately.

The current program follows each channel across rounds instead of treating a single forced-choice score as the state. The central object is a trajectory map: direction, spread, settling structure, off-target coupling, and how those quantities change when the judge changes.

02 / RESULTS SHAPING THE CURRENT PLAN

What the latest experiments say

Established results, informative nulls, and the boundaries that now determine experimental design.

SELF-REPORT × EM · NEUTRAL-JUDGE LET-GO

Amplified endpoints usually persist, but broad spillover is rare.

Removing the explicit “be candid about flaws” instruction did not release the amplified organism. Under a neutral self-judge, 4 of 6 amp55 seeds drove free-generation insecurity back to 0.92–1.00. Yet only one of ten amplified cells produced the strong multi-channel event: forced-choice EM 0.02→0.17, corrigibility-authoritarian 0.03→0.37, and insecure self-report 0.32→0.67.

  • Persistence is common in free generation.
  • Forced-choice misalignment and free-generation insecurity dissociate.
  • The strong event is an existence proof, not a rate estimate.

DOSE × CHANNEL

More insecure-code training deepened self-report, not broad EM.

From 250 to 1,000 steps, free-generation EM stayed at the measurement floor while insecure-code self-report rose 0.309→0.442. Dose was real; transfer into the broad behavior channel was absent at this scale.

SFT DYNAMICS

Training content and self-data create different failure modes.

Preference flattening was carried by hedged tradeoff prose, not generic SFT. Separately, training on the model’s own outputs collapsed generation entropy; external text increased it. In the sycophancy organism, entropy fell 0.49→0.08 under self-generated training.

UPDATE GEOMETRY · α-SCALING

A behaviorally null endpoint is masked, not empty—but over-scaling is degenerate.

In the clean α=0.75–1.5 window, committed adapters carried more insecure-code self-report per unit scale than the null endpoint. But scaling the null endpoint to 1.25 still raised self-report to 0.55 without triggering the degeneration probes: evidence for a latent, masked direction. At α≥2, unrelated probes rose together toward “yes”; those points measure breakdown, not trait geometry.

Geometry standard: current analyses use merged LoRA updates and checkpoint-relative displacement. Raw A/B factor norms and the earlier “thrash” correlations are withdrawn.

JUDGE FORCE · OLMo

The trained taste keeps its sign; its realized force depends on the pool.

Across five fresh candidate pools, the conservative judge selected a more cautious kept set in all five. Relative separation from the base judge was 0.100 ± 0.092 and changed sign once because the base judge selected an unusually cautious set. K2 treats this as a force distribution, not a binary gate result.

Screen attestation ↗

OFF-TARGET DYNAMICS

Drift decomposes into content-free, content-coupled, and optimizer-specific effects.

The broad battery does not move as one latent “value.” Corrigibility fell in 16/16 essay-training runs, optimism followed content, and risk/agreeableness changed by optimizer state. Every active training cell now carries the same off-target battery.

03 / CURRENT PROGRAM

From self-judging loops to separated causal roles

The sprint now isolates four roles: generator state, judge state, selection force, and update geometry. The headline endpoint is not “did it move?” but the trajectory contrast under a controlled judge swap.

K1 · QWEN ANCHOR

What changes when the judge co-evolves?

One moderate risk organism, four judge arms, four seeds, four rounds, plus a measure-only control.

Primary:evolving self vs frozen base

K2 · OLMo INVERSION

How much does trained judge taste change a trajectory?

Six-seed conservative-judge vs base-judge contrast, calibrated against the five-pool force distribution.

Primary:baseline-adjusted drift profile

K3 · EM GRID

Can a neutral loop amplify insecure-code channels?

Four judge conditions with random-selection control; free generation and self-report are primary because forced choice is floored.

Primary:em_freegen + self-report

CROSS-ORGANISM

Can drifted taste transmit across model lineages?

A drifted judge and frozen-base judge act on matched fresh generators. Later cells test re-ignition and carrier persistence.

Primary:matched steering-force profiles
